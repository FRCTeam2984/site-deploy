By Lukas & Marshall (I promise not to parody Sherlock Holmes book titles) on September 21st, 2018

With the continued existence of our robotics team, we continue to work out the kinks to prepare for battle at the border. Having passed club day, we are in full scale prep mode for the upcoming competition with way too much Caravan Palace. Should any questions arise from this blog post, I blame Marshall.

A new mentor has joined us, Eric Xiao a computer science major from UCSD who is mentoring our electrical and software team, I’m cool boy

“Hey guys join team 253 we have boba ;)” - Eric Xiao
